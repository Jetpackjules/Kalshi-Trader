The following strategies were implemented but removed from the active backtester on 2025-12-16 to focus on core logic.

1. MartingaleStrategy
   - Logic: Doubles risk after a losing day, resets after a winning day.
   - Goal: Recover losses quickly.
   - Risk: Exponential drawdown.

2. MeanReversionStrategy
   - Logic: Fades extreme moves. Buys YES if NO > 80 (Overbought), Buys NO if NO < 20 (Oversold).
   - Goal: Capture mean reversion in range-bound markets.

3. MomentumStrategy
   - Logic: Follows trend relative to a Moving Average (period=30).
   - Buy NO if Price > MA + 5.
   - Buy YES if Price < MA - 5.

4. CloserStrategy
   - Logic: Enters at 3 PM (15:00) if price is uncertain (40-60).
   - Goal: Bet on late-day volatility or resolution.

5. ContrarianStrategy
   - Logic: "Fade the Crash". If price drops >10c in 10 mins, BUY.
   - Goal: Catch falling knives / overreactions.

6. SniperStrategy (Volatility Breakout)
   - Logic: Wait for squeeze (range < 5c), then buy breakout.
   - Note: Was a placeholder, not fully implemented.

7. TurtleStrategy
   - Logic: "Breakout". Buy if Price > 60-minute High.
   - Goal: Catch sustained trends.

8. FOMOStrategy
   - Logic: "Momentum". If price rises >10c in 10 mins, BUY.
   - Goal: Chase fast moves.

9. LongshotStrategy
   - Logic: "Deep Value". Buy NO if Price < 20c.
   - Goal: High risk, high reward lottery tickets.
